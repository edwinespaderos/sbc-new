PROGRESSIVE & INNOVATIVE BEVERAGES

STOUT BREWING COMPANY

Stout Brewing Company was founded in 2012 with the vision to set itself apart from the competition and create something completely different using innovative brewing and packaging solutions. Stout Brewing Company is focused on providing adult beverage consumers with innovative brands that put twists on classic standards. With three brands currently distributed in over 25 states, Stout Brewing Company will continue to provide inventive products in the Flavored Malt Beverage Specialty category.

Great America

Flavored Malt Beverage Specialty

Brewed and bottled in the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains. Great America was born from Southern tradition and raised by a new breed of greatness. Available in seven flavors at 6%, 12%, and 14% ABVs, this beverage is a throwback to the past combined with a modern attitude, giving it a flavor all it’s own.

Wholesale and Retail Partnerships

Stout Brewing Company partners with both large and small distributors throughout the United States to distribute our brands in over 25 states.

Committed to the growth of our brands, as well as the relationships with our wholesale and distributor partners, we strive to develop customized programs that enhance our brands’ performance in the marketplace. We recognize that our distributors often face challenges, such as SKU rationalization, and our philosophy is to work directly with our partners to overcome these hurdles. We utilize Area Managers and Supplier Representatives to assist our distributor partners, ensuring brand success. Let us show you what a great relationship with Stout Brewing Company looks like and see how our brand portfolio can offer you profitable solutions in the growing alcoholic beverage industry. If you’d like to learn more about how we can partner together, we’d love to hear from you.
